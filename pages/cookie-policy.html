Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They enable websites to remember your preferences, measure engagement, and deliver relevant content and advertisements.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality including login, subscription access, and security. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve content and performance. Provided by Google Analytics and similar services.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by our advertising partners including Outbrain to serve relevant native content recommendations and measure campaign performance.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings such as newsletter preferences, display options, and consent choices.

Third-Party Cookies

Our advertising partners — including Outbrain, Google, and others — may set their own cookies when their content or advertisements are served on our platform. These cookies are subject to the respective privacy policies of each partner. You can opt out of interest-based advertising at aboutads.info or networkadvertising.org.

Managing Cookie Preferences

You can manage or withdraw your cookie consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting our site, where the consent banner will reappear. Most browsers also allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our services.

GDPR & CCPA Compliance

We obtain explicit consent before deploying non-essential cookies to visitors from GDPR-covered jurisdictions (European Economic Area and UK). California residents under CCPA have the right to opt out of sale of personal data (we do not sell data) and to request disclosure of data collected via cookies. Contact privacy@federaltimes.com to exercise these rights.

Cookie Consent Management

When you first visit The Federal Times, you are presented with a cookie consent banner offering Accept All, Reject, and Customize options. Your selection is stored in your browser's localStorage and honored on subsequent visits. You may change your preference at any time by clearing your browser storage.
