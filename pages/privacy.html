Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Introduction

The Federal Times ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you visit our website, subscribe to our services, or interact with our content.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including name, email address, phone number, billing information, and communication preferences when you subscribe or register. We also automatically collect browsing data, IP addresses, device identifiers, cookie data, and usage analytics through standard web technologies.

  • Account registration and subscription information
  • Newsletter signup and email engagement data
  • Contact form submissions and customer service communications
  • Payment and billing information (processed via PCI-compliant third parties)
  • Device, browser, and session analytics data

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve our services, deliver newsletters and breaking news alerts you request, process payments securely, analyze site performance and content engagement, serve contextually relevant advertisements through partners including Outbrain, and comply with applicable legal obligations.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We share information with service providers who assist in operating our platform (email delivery, payment processing, analytics), advertising partners for native content delivery and attribution, and regulatory or law enforcement entities when legally required.

Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data; opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data); withdraw consent for data processing based on consent; and lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@federaltimes.com.

Data Retention

We retain subscriber data for the duration of your subscription and for up to 24 months following account closure for legal compliance purposes. Analytics data is retained in aggregated, anonymized form indefinitely.

Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include SSL/TLS encryption, access controls, regular security audits, and staff data-protection training.

Contact

For privacy-related inquiries: privacy@federaltimes.com | 1400 K Street NW, Suite 800, Washington, D.C. 20005
