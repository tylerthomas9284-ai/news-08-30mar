FAQ
Common Questions
Can I cancel my subscription at any time? ▼
Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. If you cancel, you'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans with no questions asked.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely through our PCI-compliant payment partners. We do not store your full card number on our servers.
Can I share my subscription with family members? ▼
Individual subscriptions are for single-user access only. If your household needs access for multiple members, please contact our subscriptions team at subscriptions@federaltimes.com for household plan options.
Is the Insider plan available for organizations? ▼
Yes. We offer institutional licensing for newsrooms, think tanks, corporations, and academic institutions. Institutional licenses include multi-seat access, API data feeds, and dedicated account management. Contact advertising@federaltimes.com for enterprise pricing.
